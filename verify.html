Verify without us, anytime, forever

Every TruthStamp proof can be verified without TruthStamp existing. Even if our website disappears tomorrow, your proof is still valid. Here's how anyone can verify it directly on MegaETH:

  1. Calculate the SHA-256 hash of your file or text. Most operating systems can do this — on Mac/Linux: shasum -a 256 yourfile.pdf.
  2. Visit any MegaETH block explorer like megaexplorer.xyz and look up the transaction hash from your proof certificate.
  3. Compare the hash stored on-chain with your calculated hash. If they match — your proof is real, your timestamp is real, and nobody can change it.
